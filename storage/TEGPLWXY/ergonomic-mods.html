About Ergonomic Keyboard Mods

These pages describe a selection of useful mods, optimizations and remappings that can be applied to the standard keyboard to give a more comfortable and efficient typing experience.

The mods presented here are commonly used by members of the Colemak Forum, but they can easily be implemented by Qwerty (or indeed any other layout) users also.
